Expectations

Goals for Term 1

  1. Each group has at least one student with a solid grasp of coding in C and RobotC
  2. Each group has a fully constructed robot with:
    1. Two colour sensors to detect the line and green corners
    2. One touch sensor to detect a water tower
    3. A structurally sound body that can withstand a small drop, has a good weight distribution and properly positioned sensors
  3. Each group has functional code that can:
    1. Follow a black line on white background
    2. Turn on green corners
    3. Rotate around a water tower when the touch sensor is activated
    4. Well styled code with useful functions
